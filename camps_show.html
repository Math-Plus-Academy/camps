LEGO Robotics

Introduce your child to the wonder of robotics.

Limit 8 students.
Monday through Friday - Half-day (9am - 12pm or 12:30 - 3:30pm)

Each child will learn robotics, programming, and problem-solving while they design, build, and test LEGO robots.

LEGO Robotics gives kids the opportunity to explore engineering, math and science in a fun and engaging way! Each day students develop teamwork skills while they work with a partner on a new design challenge. Challenges include a drag race competition and more!

What will they do?

Goals:

  • Be able to describe what a “robot” is and how they impact our lives daily
  • Understand how to build and program NXT Robots using the touch, light, sound and ultrasonic sensors
  • To show perseverance and not give up when a problem is challenging

Daily Rundown:

  • What is a robot?
  • Explore LEGO parts and sensors
  • Explore 5 step programs
  • Discover LEGO Mindstorm's programming environment.
  • Discuss the ultra sonic sensor
  • Compete in build challenges.
  • And much much more!
